About Luneth SMP

Welcome to Luneth SMP, a survival multiplayer server inspired by the mysteries of the night sky. Here, players come together under the glowing crescent moon to build, explore, and create unforgettable stories.

Luneth SMP is more than just a world — it’s a community. Whether you’re a builder, explorer, redstone engineer, or adventurer, there’s a place for you here. Together we thrive in a world shaped by teamwork, creativity, and imagination.

Join us as we craft our legacy beneath the stars. The moon is always watching — guiding us through our journey in Luneth SMP.
